# Subject Area OMVS

The Subject Area Open Metadata View Service (OMVS) provides a REST API to support user interfaces (UIs)
relating to the definition and organization of subject matter expertise. 

Valuable expertise about your organization's data and operations is available across your organization. 
This expertise will not be there indefinitely as individuals move on to new roles or leave the organization. 
Subject areas define the topic areas where the gathering of subject matter expertise is needed to enhance 
the organization's capability. 

The Subject Area OMVS provides APIs for defining the subject areas you need to focus on and organizing 
the resulting knowledge:

* **Subject Area Management**: Tools for creating, updating, and deleting subject area definitions. 
  These are managed as collections, allowing for flexible organization and metadata enrichment.
* **Subject Area Hierarchies**: Capabilities for linking subject areas into hierarchies to represent 
  broader and nested topic areas, providing a structured way to navigate subject matter expertise.
